FAQ'S

1. What is EduScan?

EduScan is an educational platform dedicated to providing quality and animated educational content for primary students, particularly in rural areas.

2. Who can benefit from EduScan?

EduScan is designed to benefit primary students, teachers, parents, and educational institutions seeking innovative and engaging learning resources.

3. How does animated content benefit student learning?

Animated content on EduScan brings educational concepts to life, making learning more interactive, engaging, and memorable for students of all ages.

4. What subjects does EduScan cover?

EduScan covers a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, General Knowledge and more, tailored to primary school curriculum standards.

5. Is EduScan suitable for remote learning?

Yes, EduScan leverages technology to facilitate remote learning, enabling students to access educational content from anywhere with an internet connection.

6. Can schools integrate EduScan into their existing curriculum?

Yes, schools can seamlessly integrate EduScan into their existing curriculum, complementing traditional teaching methods with interactive and engaging digital resources.

7. Is EduScan completely free to use?

Yes, EduScan offers a free basic version with access to a limited range of educational content and features.

8. How can I provide feedback or suggestions for improvement to EduScan?

Users can provide feedback and suggestions for improvement directly through the EduScan platform or by contacting the support team via email or phone.
